What we do?
Our main aim is to provide individuals with the ability to locate rescue homes nearby and facilitate the process of evacuation with organizations looking to set up rescue homes by displaying proper locations. We use various parameters at work like rain and water level among other factors and geographical locations are filtered based upon these factors. We try to provide a complete rescue solution in case of disaster by doing so.
What are the benefits to an individual?
In the grave hours of need, we work on sharing your status with the relief organizations so that they can set up relief camps in your proximity and help you in every way possible fulfilling your concerns by giving food, shelter, medicines, etc. and you can also do your bit by making your resources available and helping other victims around you fulfilling their needs.
How an organization can contribute?
We provide you with the possible locations prone to heavy flooding around and mentions the various requirements at each of these homes where you thus being able to save lives, tend to the sick, maintaining normal services by setting up relief camps, making accommodation available, providing the necessary requirements of the victims
